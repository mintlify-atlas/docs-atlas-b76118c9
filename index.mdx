CV Builder & Exporter is a browser-based React application that lets you create structured professional CV profiles, preview them in two polished templates, and download them as print-ready PDFs or portable JSON files. No backend required — everything runs locally.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a profile">
    Enter your personal details, work experience, education, and skills using the profile form. Validation ensures all required fields are correct before saving.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a template">
    From the dashboard, pick between the **Modern** template (two-column with dark sidebar) or the **Classic** template (single-column, formal typography).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview your CV">
    The editor renders a full A4-sized CV preview directly in the browser so you can see exactly how it will look when printed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export">
    Click **Download PDF** to save a print-ready PDF, or **Export JSON** to save your raw profile data for reuse or backup.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You can manage multiple profiles and switch between them at any time from the profile selector screen.
</Tip>
